Biography-----[Curriculum Vitae]
    I am currently working as a staff research scientist at Baidu Institute of Deep Learning (IDL). I am also the NLP team lead and manager.

    I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from EECS at Oregon State Univerisity under supervision of Professor Liang Huang.

    I am broadly interested in all kinds of generative models and NLP-related problems, including speech-related problems, e.g., Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Text-To-Speech synthesis (TTS), and text-based NLP problems , e.g., Machine Translation, Question Answering, Image Captioning, Summarization, etc. My goal is to build a full end-to-end system with the most advanced Machine Learning techniques to interact with users.
